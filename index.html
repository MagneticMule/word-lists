Word Lists

A growing assortment of word-lists and micro dictionaries in English. Especially suited to English language learning tasks.

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

Why?

There is an excellent range of resources out there for the English language teacher. From simple word lists based on respected corpora such as the BNC all the way to Graded Readers. Sadly, for those of us with more "computational" needs these resources tend to be in "Teacher Friendly" formats such as MSWord files, PDF documents and PowerPoint slides.

This repo attempts to gather these resources into one convenient location while converting them to a more machine friendly format, namely JSON.
